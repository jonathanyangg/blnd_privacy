Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download blnd from the App Store and tap "Sign Up." You'll pick your favorite genres, rate a few movies, and create your account.

How do recommendations work?

blnd uses your ratings and taste profile to suggest movies you'll love. The more you rate, the better your recommendations get. In groups (Blends), recommendations are based on everyone's combined taste.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and tap the "+" button. Search for your friend's username and send them a request.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Settings > Account and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. If you need help, contact us at jonathan.yang.x@gmail.com.
